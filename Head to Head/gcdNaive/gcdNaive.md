Calculate the [Greatest Common Divisor(GCD)](keyword://greatest-common-divisor) of two numbers.

**Example**

For `a = 4` and `b = 6`, the output should be
`gcdNaive(a, b) = 2`.

**Input/Output**

*   **[time limit] 4000ms (js)**

*   **[input] integer a**

    _Guaranteed constraints:_
    `1 ≤ a ≤ 500`.

*   **[input] integer b**

    _Guaranteed constraints:_
    `1 ≤ b ≤ 500`.

*   **[output] integer**

    _GCD_ of `a` and `b`.
