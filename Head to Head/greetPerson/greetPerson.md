Greet a person given their name.

**Example**

For `name = "Polly"`, the output should be
`greetPerson(name) = "Hello, Polly"`.

**Input/Output**

*   **[time limit] 4000ms (js)**

*   **[input] string name**

    A name of a person.

    _Guaranteed constraints:_
    `3 ≤ name.length ≤ 10`.

*   **[output] string**

    Greeting in the following form "Hello, `name`".
